\subsection*{Integer Programming:  Branch and Bound}
An integer programme (IP) is to be solved using the tabular Branch and Bound method:
  
  \begin{framed}


Maximize p = 7x + 9y

\medskip

subject to
\medskip


\begin{tabular}{cl}
	

	
	
	\phantom{space}


	& 2x-y $\leq$\; 40\\
	
	& 2x + 3y $\leq$\; 90\\

	
	& x,y $\geq$\; 0\\
	
	& x,y are integers\\
	
	
	
\end{tabular}
\end{framed}
